Product engineering approach

How I would turn customer integrations into Minicor product improvements

The product challenge is not just automating a screen. It is making the workflow reliable, observable, debuggable, recoverable, reusable and easier for future customers to launch.

1

Observe

Work directly on customer integrations, map the desktop workflow, capture failure modes, edge cases, human workarounds and environment constraints.

2

Stabilize

Build guardrails for UI state, retries, validation, logging, timeouts, recovery actions and clear operator feedback.

3

Abstract

Identify repeated patterns across implementations and convert them into reusable workflow primitives and developer-facing APIs.

4

Ship

Release small product improvements quickly, validate them with forward-deployed engineers and customers, and iterate based on production behavior.

5

Scale

Improve developer experience, observability, debugging, documentation and onboarding so future integrations go live faster.

The role needs a product-minded engineer who can work closely with customers, ship features, improve developer experience, and turn repeated implementation problems into reusable abstractions.

What the role does
